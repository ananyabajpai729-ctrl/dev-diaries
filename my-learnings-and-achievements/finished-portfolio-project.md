## Just wrapped up my Portfolio project and the feeling is surreal**

I began as someone who hardly had any idea how websites get all these colours and images and styles.

Someone who knew only basic HTML5, studied and forgotten in class 7th.

Chai aur code's playlist went a long way in strengthening my HTML and CSS knowledge and this project became the de-facto execution of that.

### Things I did well
- **Clean & Modern Dark Theme** with smooth navigation and responsive design across all devices.
- **Fully Responsive** – Optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewing.
- **Interactive Project Cards** showcasing my Machine Learning and Data Science projects.
- Skills Section with neatly organized capsules for Programming Languages, Web Development, ML/DL tools, and more.
- Direct **Clickable Links** to GitHub, LinkedIn, LeetCode, and Email.
- **Minimalist & Fast Loading** – Built for great user experience and quick navigation.
- **Professional Layout** with clear sections: About, Skills, Projects, Certifications, and Contact.
