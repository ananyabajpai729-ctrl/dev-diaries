Understanding when and why to use techniques like:

* Hashing
* Two Pointers

* Recursion

Maintaining my DSA repository and writing explanations for solutions has helped me identify gaps in my understanding and strengthen core concepts.

### 🔹 Small Efforts Compound Over Time

Even on busy days, solving a single problem or reviewing a concept keeps the momentum alive. Consistent effort adds up.
