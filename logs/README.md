# Learning Logs

This folder contains short notes from my day-to-day learning journey.

These aren't tutorials or detailed explanations. They're simply snapshots of what I learned, what confused me, what finally clicked, and sometimes even what didn't go as planned.

The goal isn't to document every topic I study. Instead, I try to capture the one idea or realization that changed the way I think. Looking back at these logs should remind me not just *what* I learned, but *how* I learned it.

Some entries may be about DSA, others about AI Engineering, web development, Git, competitive programming, or anything else I happen to be exploring at the time.

Think of this as my engineering journal—one day at a time.
